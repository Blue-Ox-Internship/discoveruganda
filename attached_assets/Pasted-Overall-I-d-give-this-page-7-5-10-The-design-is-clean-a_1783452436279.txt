Overall, I'd give this page **7.5/10**. The design is clean and professional, but it doesn't yet convince a hotel or tourism business **why they should invest money**. Right now it looks like a sponsorship page. It needs to feel like a **business opportunity**.

Here are my detailed thoughts.

---

# What's Working Well

## 1. Clean Branding ✅

The colors immediately feel like tourism and nature.

The typography is professional.

The page looks trustworthy.

## Overall: **8/10**

The page looks polished, but it currently explains **what** Speak Uganda is rather than **why a business should invest**. Shift the messaging from *"here are our partnership options"* to *"here's how we generate bookings, visibility, and revenue for your business."* If you add the customer journey, real card mockups, QR-code flow, partner benefits, and a clearer value proposition, this could easily become a **9.5/10 partnership page** that is much more persuasive to hotels, lodges, tour operators, and tourism brands.
